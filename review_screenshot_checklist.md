# App Store Screenshot Compliance Checklist

Verify that all screenshots uploaded to the App Store for marketing purposes comply with the review-safe experience guidelines.

## Forbidden Visual Elements

* [ ] **No Pricing**: Ensure no image shows subscription amounts (e.g., $4.99/month, $29.99/year, $59.99 lifetime).
* [ ] **No Paywalls**: Do not capture any view of the paywall interface or subscription packages.
* [ ] **No subscription labels**: Avoid terms like "Monthly Subscription", "Annual Subscription", or "Lifetime Access".
* [ ] **No "PRO" badges**: Verify that no crowns, PRO tags, or premium account banners appear in profile headers or list views.
* [ ] **No "Free Trial" text**: Ensure no references to trial offerings or promotional durations are visible.
* [ ] **No "Dev Bypass" tags**: Check that no development or diagnostic controls appear in screen layouts.
* [ ] **No purchase buttons**: Confirm that "Purchase Subscription" and "Restore Purchases" controls are absent.
* [ ] **No locked features**: Avoid displaying greyed-out buttons, locks, or keys indicating gated access.

## Allowed Elements (Review-Safe Screenshots Only)

* [ ] **Polished Reagent Lists**: Show lists of standard chemical reagents (e.g., Reagent A, Reagent B) with safety badge tags.
* [ ] **Complete Test History**: Display populated log lists showing historical observations and chemical reports.
* [ ] **Detailed Chemical Info**: Capture reaction details, storage recommendations, and safety handling info.
* [ ] **Clean Settings Profile**: Showcase basic profiles and scientific settings options only.
